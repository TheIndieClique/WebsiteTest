About Us

The Indie Clique was founded in 2019 by four friends from college, trying to stay connected through video games. What initially began as simple discussions on games quickly turned into a series of full-blown game reviews. In addition to game reviews, we’ll be posting a variety of other content including individual reviews of other media, op-eds, and hey - maybe even podcast! We hope you’ll take a look around and enjoy what you find here.
